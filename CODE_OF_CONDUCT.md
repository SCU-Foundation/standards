# SCU Foundation Standards Repository — Code of Conduct

The SCU Foundation is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and professional environment for all contributors.

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within this repository and related communication channels.

The canonical repository index is:

- REPO: Repo‑Map — /REPO-MAP-vE1.1.md
- REPO: Repo‑Map — /GRM-vE1.1.json

---

## Our Standards

Contributors MUST:

- treat others with respect  
- provide constructive, evidence‑based feedback  
- avoid personal attacks or inflammatory language  
- maintain professionalism in all discussions  
- respect the neutrality and non‑commercial nature of the standards  

Contributors MUST NOT:

- harass, insult, or belittle others  
- introduce discriminatory or exclusionary behavior  
- derail discussions with unrelated or hostile commentary  
- promote proprietary or commercial agendas  

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- warnings  
- temporary or permanent bans from participation  
- escalation to SCU Foundation governance  

Enforcement decisions are made by SCU Foundation maintainers.

---

## Reporting

To report violations, open an issue marked **“Code of Conduct”** or contact the SCU Foundation through:

https://scu.foundation

All reports will be handled confidentially.

---

## Commitment

By participating in this repository, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
