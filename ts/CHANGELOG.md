# TS v1.0 Changelog

## v1.0 — Initial Release (Final)
- First canonical edition of the Lattice Trust Stack (TS).
- Establishes the constitutional trust, identity, safety, provenance, and interoperability primitives for the Lattice ecosystem.
- Defines the five-layer trust architecture: Domain Trust, Perceptual Trust, Runtime Safety & Memory Integrity, Identity & Attestation, and Certification & Governance.
- Removes all proprietary, product-specific, or implementation-specific elements to ensure neutrality and standards-body independence.
- Removes all references to SCU Labs and other third-party implementers; aligns institutional roles with SCU Foundation (steward) and Lattice Research Institute (research body).
- Aligns TS with the governance model, Standards Registry, and canonical surfaces of the Lattice ecosystem.
- Establishes TS as the constitutional substrate for LP, LM, LW, and LIT.
- Provides normative requirements, conformance rules, and governance/change‑management alignment.
- Includes publication‑grade formatting for PDF, HTML, and GitHub editions.
